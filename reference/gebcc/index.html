    gebcc

    General electron-boson coupled cluster.

    ebcc.gebcc.Amplitudes

    Bases: rebcc.Amplitudes

    Amplitude container class. Consists of a dictionary with keys that are strings of the name of each amplitude. Values are namespaces with keys indicating whether each fermionic dimension is alpha ("a") or beta ("b") spin, and values are arrays whose dimension depends on the particular amplitude. For purely bosonic amplitudes the values of Amplitudes are simply arrays, with no fermionic spins to index.

    ebcc.gebcc.ERIs(ebcc, array=None, slices=None, mo_coeff=None)

    Bases: rebcc.ERIs

    Electronic repulsion integral container class. Consists of a namespace containing blocks of the integrals, with keys that are length-4 strings of "o" or "v" signifying whether the corresponding dimension is occupied or virtual.
